Fall 2014 - Class 2016

Course Number: DPT 617
Course Title: SERVICE LEARNING I
Contact Hours: 3 hours per week
Credits: 1
Description: This course will provide physical therapy students with the opportunity to evaluate and serve the needs of the local community utilizing basic exam and treatment skills under faculty supervision. The students will evaluate their role as health professionals and as citizens to promote positive change within their community.
Schedule: Wednesdays and Fridays throughout Term II and III from 1:00 pm – 4:00.
Location: Schedule of rotations and student groups attached to syllabus
