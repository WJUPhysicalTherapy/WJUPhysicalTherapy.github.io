DPT 611 Basic Science II (5 crs)

Guided independent study of structures and functions of the body in healthy and impaired states, and the impact of health care evaluations and interventions on structures and functions. This includes the study of bones, joints, muscles, peripheral nerves, and energy management. Topics are integrated closely with client cases in Clinical Science II.
